## How buyers should compare companies

Before choosing a document digitization company, compare vendors on practical execution:

- Can they scan high volumes without losing file sequence?
- Can they work onsite if confidential records cannot leave the premises?
- Can they create searchable PDFs and metadata indexes?
- Can they deliver DMS, EMR, ERP or cloud-ready output?
- Do they have experience with medical, banking, legal, HR, government and finance files?
- Can they explain document movement, batch tracking and quality checks?
- Do they have case studies or examples of similar work?

The lowest per-page rate is not always the best choice. Poor indexing, missed pages, weak file naming or unclear output can make retrieval difficult after the scanning project is over.
